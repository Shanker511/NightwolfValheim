41 Of The Most Hilarious Amazon Reviews Ever

Lina D.
BoredPanda staff
Publish

Not your original work? Add source

Declaring our opinion about this or that is nothing new - though usually new books, movies or music were reviewed in newspapers or serious TV shows by professionals, we've entered the glory age of evaluating by the use of the internet. Now every one of us can share our experiences not even after reading a book or watching a movie, but also about an ink pen, banana slicer, and arts & crafts glitter. And what is the best place to scrutinize if not the online shops?

One of the biggest e-shop's Amazon client feedback provides useful information to help you decide whether to buy a particular product or not. Other product reviews are useless, like the ones where people give something a one star just because it arrived late. But there's another and the very best review type that's neither useful nor useless, but what they lack in helpfulness, they make up for in pure hilarity. There are plenty of funny Amazon reviews - like the one about a Swiss army knife, where the buyer instantly turned into a Navy Seal or a funny review where a computer mouse saves a relationship. Take a look at these best Amazon reviews to see what we mean. Compiled by Bored Panda, this original list of funniest Amazon reviews won't help you to buy anything, but it'll certainly help to put a smile on your face.
